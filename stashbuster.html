StashBuster

[Ongoing]

What is it?

StashBuster is a social, collaborative, and eco-conscious Android application where users share photos of their craft supply stashes – fabric, yarn, beads, paper, etc. – and swap supplies and ideas for using them, boosting creativity and keeping craft materials out of landfills.

Built With

Java, XML
