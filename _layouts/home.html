About -

Hi, my name is Zi Nguyen and I'm a Boston-based self-taught photographer turned web developer.

My first experience with coding came when I wanted to make some stylistic changes to my photography portfolio. Not knowing much about HTML or CSS, I embarked on several Google search adventures to find the code I needed and followed the instructions to implement them on my site. Back then, I found great power in using inline styling :)

Today, I'm a Web Developer in training. I'm a complete self-taught who's in debt to the web and people that are willing to share their knowledge. Besides coding, I also have some experience in design and am comfortable with visual software such as Photoshop, Sketch, and InDesign.

When I'm not in front of a computer or behind a camera, I love to explore new places with my husband, break a sweat at the gym, or read a good book.
