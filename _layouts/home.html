About -

Hi, my name is Zi Nguyen and I'm a Boston-based photographer turned web developer.

My first experience with coding came when I was working on my photography portfolio. Hoping to take the existing template and adjust it to fit my creative vision, I embarked on several Google adventures to learn HTML and CSS and successfully created a webpage that I loved. Back then, I found great power in using inline styling :) I felt especially empowered afterwards and before I realized, I've spent the last year and half immersing myself in learning to program.

Today, I'm a Web Developer in training. I'm a complete self-taught who's in debt to the web and people that are willing to share their knowledge. Besides coding, I also have experience in design and am comfortable with visual software such as Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign, and Sketch.

When I'm not in front of a computer or behind a camera, I love to explore new places with my husband, break a sweat at the gym, or read a good book.
