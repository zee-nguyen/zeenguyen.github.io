About -

Hi, my name is Zee and I'm a Boston-based photographer and a software engineer in the making.

My first experience with coding came a few years ago when I was working on my photography portfolio. Hoping to take the existing template and adjust it to fit my creative vision, I embarked on several Google adventures to learn HTML and CSS and successfully created a webpage that I loved. Back then, I found great power in using inline styling :) I felt especially empowered afterwards and before I realized, I've spent the last year and half immersing myself in learning to program.

Today, I'm excited to bring my experience in marketing, digital media, and visual arts into creating beautiful and meaningful applications. I enjoy the combination of creativity and problem-solving that programming brings, together with endless opportunities to learn new things each day. I'm currently pursuing my Master's Degree in Computer Science at Northeastern University's Khoury College of Computer and Information Sciences.

When I'm not in front of a computer or behind a camera, I love to explore new places, break a sweat at the gym, or read a good book.
